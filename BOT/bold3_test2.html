We're on a mission to reach every child in the world with God's life-changing Word.

BOLD3 Coffee is a 501(c)(3) Christian Nonprofit Ministry the Lord called us to while spending time with Him every morning over coffee.

As His Word filled us with boldness, He wrote Acts 1:8 on our hearts: "But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes upon you. And you will be my witnesses, telling people about me everywhere—in Jerusalem, throughout Judea, in Samaria, and to the ends of the earth.”

So we set out on a journey to craft the most ethically sourced, healthy and delicious coffee that people would be proud of drinking while spreading the best news ever.

When you buy BOLD3 Coffee you aren't just getting Heavenly Coffee, you're giving the Hope, Truth, and Love of Jesus Christ to another person. You are joining a team of passionate people who believe Jesus changes everything and want to see more of Him in hearts around the world.

We invite you to join us on this journey. Together in Christ, we can fill the world with billions of BOLD ambassadors for Jesus.

Love, the BOLD3 Team ❤︎
